#🍫 Chocolate Sales & Profitability Dashboard

An interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing chocolate shipment data to optimize inventory, identify profit drivers, and improve operational decision-making across product categories and geographic markets.

---

## 📊 Business Problem

A chocolate distribution business needed visibility into sales performance, profitability margins, and shipment trends across multiple regions and product lines. Without centralized reporting, decision-makers lacked the insights needed to:

1. **Identify high-margin products** and optimize product mix
2. **Track regional performance** to allocate resources effectively
3. **Monitor profitability trends** to adjust pricing and cost strategies
4. **Forecast demand** based on historical shipment patterns

This dashboard consolidates shipment data into a single view, enabling stakeholders to make data-driven decisions on inventory, pricing, and market expansion.

---

## 📁 Dataset Overview

The dataset contains **10,000+ shipment records** spanning multiple product categories and geographic regions. Key data points include:

- Shipment details (date, quantity, destination)
- Product information (category, SKU, pricing)
- Financial metrics (unit cost, revenue, profit margin)
- Customer and regional data

**Key attributes**: Shipment date, product category, units shipped, revenue, cost of goods sold (COGS), gross profit, region, customer segment

## 🔍 Key Insights

1. **Premium product lines account for 45% of gross profit despite representing only 20% of shipment volume**, highlighting the importance of maintaining and expanding high-margin offerings.

2. **Q4 (October–December) drives 40% of annual revenue**, with November and December alone contributing 60% of Q4 sales. Inventory planning must account for this seasonal surge.

3. **Top 5 customers generate 35% of total revenue**, indicating concentration risk. Diversifying the customer base or securing long-term contracts with key accounts should be prioritized.

4. **North America represents 45% of sales but only 32% of profit margin**, suggesting pricing or cost inefficiencies in this region. Regional cost analysis is needed.

5. **Afternoon shipments (12 PM–5 PM) account for 55% of daily volume**, creating operational bottlenecks. Spreading shipments across shifts could improve logistics efficiency.

6. **Product category "Artisan Chocolates" has the highest margin (42%) but the lowest market penetration (15% of customers)**, representing an upsell opportunity.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

**Focus on high-margin products**  
Expand marketing and production capacity for premium and artisan lines. Consider bundling high-margin items with volume products to improve overall profitability.

**Prepare for seasonal demand**  
Increase inventory by 50% ahead of Q4, particularly for top-selling products. Negotiate advance orders with suppliers to lock in favorable pricing.

**Diversify customer base**  
Launch targeted outreach to mid-tier customers to reduce dependency on top accounts. Implement tiered pricing or volume discounts to attract new buyers.

**Optimize regional pricing and costs**  
Conduct a cost audit for North America to identify inefficiencies. Consider regional pricing adjustments or shipping route optimization to improve margins.

**Balance shipment schedules**  
Incentivize morning and evening shipments through scheduling adjustments or customer delivery preferences. This can reduce peak-hour congestion and improve logistics costs.

## 🧠 Power BI Analysis

The dashboard leverages Power BI's analytical capabilities to deliver interactive insights:

- **DAX measures** calculate key metrics such as total revenue, gross profit, profit margin percentage, year-over-year growth, and customer lifetime value
- **Time intelligence functions** track monthly trends, seasonal patterns, and year-to-date performance
- **Filters and slicers** enable drill-down analysis by product category, region, time period, and customer segment
- **Conditional formatting** highlights top performers and underperforming areas at a glance
- **Visualizations** include trend lines, profitability matrices, geographic heat maps, and KPI cards for quick decision-making

The dashboard is designed for executive stakeholders, sales managers, and operations teams to monitor performance and identify opportunities in real time.
